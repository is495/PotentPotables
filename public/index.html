Classic Cocktails

Learn them. Make them. Drink them.

Martini

Shake gin with a splash of vermouth and ice. Strain into martini glass and garnish with olive.

  • 3 oz of Gin
  • 1 splash Vermouth
  • 3 olives
Old Fashioned

Add a few dashes of bitters and muddle sugar cube. Add ice cube and pour in bourbon. Serve with a stirring rod.

  • 2 oz Bourbon
  • 1 dash of Bitters
  • 1 Sugar Cube
  • 1/2 oz Water
  • Lemon Slice
  • Maraschino Cherry
  • Orange Slice
Margarita

Add tequila, lime juice, and sweet and sour into shaker with ice. Shake vigorously and strain into glass rimmed with salt. Add lime if you're feeling fancy.

  • 2 oz tequila
  • 2 oz fresh lime juice
  • 2 oz sweet and sour
  • Slice of lime
Bloody Mary

Shake ice, vodka, tomato juice, Worcestershire sauce, Horseradish, hot sauce, salt/pepper, and lemon juice. Shake and strain into a garnished glass.

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 3 oz tomato juice
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • Dash of grated horseradish
  • 3 dashes hot sauce
  • 1 dash black pepper
  • 1 pinch salt
  • celery
Mojito

Muddle mint leaves with simple syrup and lime. Add light rum, ice, and stir. Lastly top off with club soda and garnish with mint.

  • 2 oz Light Rum
  • 8 oz Club Soda
  • 1/2 oz Lime juice
  • 5 Mint Leaves
  • 1 oz Simple Syrup
Moscow Mule

Pour lime juice into Collins glass. Add ice cubes and pour in ginger beer. Vodka optional and serve with a stirring rod.

  • 1 1/2 oz Vodka
  • 8 oz Ginger Beer
  • 1/2 oz Lime juice
  • Lime
Lemon Drop

Rim shot glass with sugar. Shake vodka, lemon juice, and triple sec with ice. Strain into a chilled shot glass.

  • 1 1/2 oz vodka
  • 1/2 oz TripleSec
  • 1 oz Lemon Juice
  • Sugar Rim
Long Island Ice Tea

Shake ice, vodka, tequila, rum, gin, triple sec, and sweet and sour mix. Strain into garnished glass and top with cola.

  • 1/2 oz Gin
  • 1/2 oz Rum
  • 1/2 oz Tequila
  • 1/2 oz Triple Sec
  • 1 1/2 oz Vodka
  • 1 oz Cola
  • 1 oz Sweet and Sour
  • Lemon Wedge
Whiskey Sour

Shake whiskey, lemon juice, and simple syrup with ice. Strain into a chilled glass and garnish.

  • 1 1/2 oz Whiskey
  • 3/4 oz Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 oz Simple Syrup
  • Maraschino Cherry
Gin and Tonic

Add gin into a glass fill with ice. Stir in tonic water and garnish.

  • 2 oz Gin
  • 3 oz Tonic Water
  • Slice of Lime
AMF

Pour everything into a chilled glass except the soda. Gently pour in the soda and stir.

  • 1 oz Vodka
  • 1 1/2 oz Blue Curacao
  • 1 oz Gin
  • 1 oz Light Rum
  • 1 oz Tequila
  • 1 oz Lemon Lime Soda
  • 2 oz Sweet and Sour
  • Maraschino Cherry
Daquiri

Shake all ingredients with ice and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

  • 1 1/2 oz White Rum
  • 1 oz Lime Juice
  • 1/2 oz Simple Syrup
  • Slice of Lime
Rum and Cola

Pour rum into a highball glass filled with ice. Top with cola and garnish with a lime wedge.

  • 1 2/3 oz White Rum
  • 4 oz Cola
  • 1/3 oz Lime Juice
  • Slice of Lime
Redbull Vodka

Pour vodka into a highball glass filled with ice. Top with Redbull until full. Lightly mix and serve.

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 1 can of Redbull
Whiskey Cola

Pour whiskey into a lowball glass filled with ice. Top with cola. Lightly mix and serve.

  • 2 oz Whiskey
  • 10 oz Cola
Screw Driver

Pour vodka into a glass. Top with orange juice until full. Lightly mix and serve.

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 4 oz Orange Juice
  • Orange Slice
Holiday Nog

Pour Peppermint Schnapps into glass. Top with Eggnog until full. Lightly mix and serve.

  • 1 oz Peppermint Schnapps
  • 4 oz Eggnog
  • Dash of Nutmeg
Hot Toddy

Coat bottom of mug with honey. Add shot of bourbon, lemon juice, and fill with hot water. Stur until honey is dissolved and serve.

  • 1 oz Bourbon
  • 1 tsp Honey
  • 2 tsp Lemon Juice
  • Lemon Slice
