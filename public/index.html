Classic Cocktails

Learn them. Make them. Drink them.

Vodka Martini

Vodka replaces gin in this variation of the classic martini. Shake until ice cold and serve up in a chilled martini glass.

  • 3 fluid ounces vodka
  • 1 fluid ounce dry vermouth
  • 3 olives
Old Fashioned

The Old Fashioned is a cocktail made by muddling sugar with bitters, with bourbon, and a twist of citrus rind.

  • 1 1/2 oz Bourbon
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 1 Sugar cube
  • Few dashes plain water
  • Citrus rind
Manhattan

A Manhattan is a cocktail made with whiskey, sweet vermouth, and bitters. Commonly used whiskeys include rye, Canadian whisky, bourbon, blended whiskey, and Tennessee whiskey.

  • 2 Parts Whiskey
  • 2 Dashes Bitters
  • 1 Part Vermouth
  • 2 Maraschino Cherries
  • Citrus rind
